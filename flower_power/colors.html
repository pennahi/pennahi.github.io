Discussion of color

I really like colors! They make earth a brighter place!

I like colorful flowers and I often pick flowers with a certain color theme at a time.

  • Sunflowers are yellow
  • Roses can be almost any color, but I like the red ones the most
  • Wood anemones are white and they are a sign of spring here

Sometimes I buy cut flowers from the store,
but I can't keep any actual house plant flowers alive

But typical greener house plants survive well.
I don't know what's up with those.

Flower power!
