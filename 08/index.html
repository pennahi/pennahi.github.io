When and where


The ceremony will be held at 2 o'clock on February 14th, 2026, at Helsinki's Old Church (Lönnrotinkatu 6, 00120 Helsinki).

After the ceremony the reception will be held at the Banquet Hall of Restaurant Sipuli (Kanavaranta 7, 00160 Helsinki)

Dress code: Semi-Formal

Timetable


CHURCH: The ceremony will he held at the Old Church of Helsinki and will take around half an hour to an hour of time.

After that you are welcome to join us on a tour ride with the KOFF tram around Helsinki until we reach our reception destination at Restaurant Sipuli.

SIPULI Our reception will be held in the Banquet Hall on the third floor of the building where we will be until Midnight. You can use stairs or an elevator to reach the third floor, then turn right.

AFTERPARTY will take place in Le Bloom (Eteläesplanadi 12, Helsinki)

FOOD & DRINKS

Food will be in a buffet style and it will have a varied collection of different kinds of foods
But if you have any dietary restrictions we and the staff of Sipuli would want to know about it in advance!

This is a restaurant with A-rights, which means you can have alcohol from the restaurant, so please leave your own drinks at home!
Alcohol-free options are also thought out.

GOOD TO KNOW


About accessability You can go between floors with an elevator but to reach the actual Banquet Hall, you need to be able to walk down a short staircase of three steps.

Toilets will be on the first floor, left of the main entrance.

Drinks during the buffet will be paid by the couple but after that you are able to purchase beverages from the bar by yourself.

Bridal Party Contacts are Pia (+358 401234567) and Eppu (+358 401234567) if you need to get a hold of someone, since the bride and groom will not be carrying phones with them.
